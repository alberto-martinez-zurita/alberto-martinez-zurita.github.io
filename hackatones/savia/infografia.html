The Financial Inclusion Gap

Financial literacy isn't a one-size-fits-all problem. Different groups face unique barriers, requiring a tailored, empathetic approach rather than a generic chatbot.

Gen Z: The Inexperienced

Digital natives who are susceptible to online misinformation and need foundational concepts explained in an authentic, non-condescending tone.

The Elderly: The Vulnerable

Facing a dual threat of digital exclusion and a high risk of targeted financial fraud, they require simple, safe, and respectful systems.

SAVIA's Proactive Coaching Model

Our innovation is an AI that doesn't just answer questions, but actively protects and educates users based on their unique vulnerabilities through an adaptive, multi-stage process.
