Welcome to SAVIA

SAVIA is a project conceived to revolutionize financial education through artificial intelligence. Our mission is to create an AI financial advisor that is inclusive, reliable, and accessible to everyone, especially vulnerable populations. This interactive dashboard summarizes our strategic plan, designed for a high-impact hackathon but with a clear vision towards a scalable and compliant product under strict European regulations.

The name SAVIA, which in Spanish evokes the vitality of plants and phonetically sounds like "sabia" (wise), encapsulates our mission: to nurture financial knowledge intelligently and accessibly. Browse the tabs to discover how we address the challenge, our technical solution, the action plan, and our vision for the future.

The Challenge: Real Financial Inclusion

Our solution focuses on breaking down real barriers for two key user profiles with very different needs.

Comparison of Financial Barriers

Gen Z

Inexperienced and exposed to misinformation. They need basic concepts and to distinguish reliable sources from speculative ones.

SAVIA Solution: Adaptive RAG with educational sources and proactive guardrails against "guaranteed high return" promises.

The Elderly

They face digital exclusion and high vulnerability to fraud. They require simple interfaces and active protection.

SAVIA Solution: High-contrast UI, voice options, and alerts for sensitive personal data requests.

Compliance by Design: EU AI Act

SAVIA is classified as a "high-risk AI system" under Regulation (EU) 2024/1689. This implies strict obligations that we integrate from the start.

Art. 9: Risk Management

Maintain a continuous system to identify and mitigate risks like inaccurate advice.

Art. 10: Data Governance

Use reliable and documented data sources (e.g., Bank of Spain, CNMV) for the RAG.

Art. 12: Record-Keeping

Automatic logging of every interaction to ensure traceability of results.

Art. 14: Human Oversight

Design that allows a human to supervise, intervene, and override the system's responses.

Art. 50: Transparency

Always inform the user that they are interacting with an AI and explain its limitations.

Demonstrable Reliability

Our "wow factor" is a validation script that proves 93-98% accuracy against a curated test set.
